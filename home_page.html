Support's Support

This site is for new and returning supports. This site will help you determine what support character to play based on the bot laner’s pick. The site will inform you on the five best supports based on the bot laner’s pick as well as the items that are typically bought for said support character. The site will also instruct you on how the lane will typically be played. The site will also talk about why these supports work well with the bot laner.
